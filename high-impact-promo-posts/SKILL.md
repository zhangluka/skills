# High-Impact Promotional Posts

## Trigger
When writing a post that rides a trend, promotes a product/tool, or shares a piece of news across social media platforms.

## Core Principle
**Never write a post that's just information relay.** Every post must earn the reader's attention through credibility, data, or unique angle.

## Pre-Writing Research (MANDATORY — do this before writing)

1. **Find the original source** — quote key phrases from the original post/announcement. Include author name and platform.
2. **Search for third-party reviews/evaluations** — look for:
   - PhD/expert commentary (博士/研究员)
   - Popular tech reviewers (卡兹克/数字生命, etc.)
   - Viral discussions on 小红书/微信公众号
   - X/Twitter English-language discourse
3. **Collect concrete data points** — view counts, engagement numbers, specific metrics
4. **Identify the hook** — what's surprising, counterintuitive, or uniquely valuable?

### Search Channels (check ALL before writing)

| Channel | What to look for | How to search |
|---------|-----------------|---------------|
| X/Twitter | Original post, English-language expert takes | Search product/tool name, check quote tweets |
| 小红书 | Chinese reviews, user experiences, 测评 | Search product name + 测评/体验/实测 |
| 微信公众号 | In-depth analysis, 博士/专家 articles | Search product name + 公众号 |
| 今日头条 | News coverage, trending discussions | Search product name |
| YouTube | Video reviews, tutorials | Search product name + review/test |
| GitHub | Star count, issues, community activity | Check repo directly |

**Always cite**: original author + at least one third-party evaluation. This is what separates credible content from 水文.

## Writing Structure

### Hook (first 2 lines)
- Use data or a counterintuitive statement
- Example: "25万浏览量的推文，就因为小米送了100T tokens"

### Credibility Layer
- Quote the original author directly
- Reference third-party reviews with attribution
- Example: "卡兹克的数字生命做过实测，结论是..."

### Value Content
- The actual information — but framed around "why this matters to YOU"
- Not just features, but implications

### Engagement Closer
- Question or call-to-action
- Hashtags (platform-specific)

## Platform Adaptations

### 小红书
- Emoji-heavy titles, visual language
- Personal tone ("救命！程序员疯了吧")
- 8-12 hashtags

### 微信公众号 (谷雨计划)
- Formal but engaging
- Quote blocks for third-party sources
- Deeper analysis, fewer emojis

### 今日头条
- News-style, informational
- SEO-friendly titles with keywords
- Structured with clear sections

### X/Twitter
- English, concise
- Tag relevant accounts
- Link to source

## Pitfalls
- ❌ Just listing features/info — this is "水文" (low-quality content)
- ❌ Writing without researching first — you'll miss credibility hooks
- ❌ Ignoring the original author — quoting them = their audience sees your post
- ❌ Same content across all platforms — must adapt tone and structure
- ❌ Forgetting to check third-party reviews — PhD/expert quotes dramatically increase persuasiveness
